About Me

Born in India in 1999. I obtained a degree in Electrical Engineering from Manipal Institute of Technology with a minor in Fundamentals of Computing. Currently, I am pursuing a master's degree in Computational Linguistics at the University of Washington, Seattle. I have worked in various roles in the industry, including as a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Babbel, and have co-founded an Ed-Tech startup called DigiTutor.

Research Interests

Natural Language Processing, Speech Processing, Machine Learning, and Agile Development. My graduate studies focus on computational linguistics, specifically on the development of novel algorithms for semantic search and entity detection.
