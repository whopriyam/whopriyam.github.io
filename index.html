About Me

Hi there! I am Priyam, currently a Computational Linguistics grad student at the University of Washington, Seattle. Previously I have worked at various organizations on speech pronunciation evaluation, document processing and healthcare. I also have research experience in privacy-enabled NLP and data augmentation for low-resource domains with publications in EMNLP and ICML. My interests are in machine learning, primarily focusing on speech processing for AI in healthcare and accessibility.
